Hi, I'm Roshni. From the Sanskrit language, Roshni means brightness or light. I like to create changes that help us live more meaningful lives.

I'm passionate about shared experiences that are accessible to many people and deepen connections between people. Examples are public art, mass transit, and open internet.

Most recently, I was Founder & CEO of Impart. Before that, I was a former consultant and project manager for Mercer and New York City Transit. I also like to paint, read, and play sports.

To get in touch, email me at roshnishah.rps@gmail.com. I love meeting and learning from other people.
